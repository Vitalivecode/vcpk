The Placement cell holds the objective of acquainting the students of the Institute with Industries, Hospitals and other health care systems. The Placement cell organizes and extends services towards collaborative placement activities. The cell conducts training activities for the students mainly focusing on Career planning, Personality Development and Communication skills to face interviews. The placement cell works to built Industry- Institute Interaction, in-plant training, Industrial visits, On-campus or off-campus placements, Entrepreneurship Development and maintains the fruitful environment with industry and hospital.

Functions of Placement Cell

  • Collection of Data of the students and companies, maintaining and updating the database
  • Preparing eligible pool of students of different disciplines
  • Gathering information about job-fairs and employment opportunities
  • Notifying the students about Pre-Placement Activities, Job Opportunities and Campus Selections through Notice Boards, Placement Portal and College Website
  • Organizing Campus Recruitment Training Programme
  • Networking with leading companies for Internships and Campus Selections
  • Organizing on and Off Campus Interviews
  • Acts as an interface between the selected students and the recruiters
  • Conducting Career Guidance Sessions
  • Collecting Feedback from the Recruiters and Students
  • To do post placement analysis and take remedial measures for non-selected students

Objectives:

  • To guide students to choose right career and to give knowledge, skill, aptitude and meet the manpower requirements of the Industry.
  • To assist students to develop/clarify their academic career interests, their short and long-term goals through individual counseling and group sessions
  • To assist students for industrial training at the end of the fourth and sixth year.
  • To provide resources and activities to facilitate the career planning process.
  • To act as a link between students, alumni, and the employment community
  • To assist students in obtaining placement in reputed companies

Vision:

To tailor young minds become nonpareil and equip them with significant and conceptualized professional skills for realizing their best possible career path .

Mission:

  • To up skill students towards domain specific knowledge and skill sets
  • To ingrain soft skills in students
  • To develop employability skills among the students
  • To develop and strengthen industry- institute interaction
  • Maximize the number student placements through on-campus or off- campus placement drives
