Pharm. D course was established in the year 2013. Pharmacy is the profession that connects health sciences with chemical sciences. With the increased number of pharmaceutical products and introduction of newer therapeutic options, there is an increased complexity of information regarding selection of appropriate therapy. Therefore, it is "need of the hour" to make the pharmacy profession an integral part of health care system for safe and effective use of medicines. This increased need has led to development of novel dimensions in pharmacy education like the introduction of Pharm. D programme in India.

Pharm. D (Doctor of Pharmacy) is a professional doctorate degree in pharmacy. In developed countries, it is the minimum requirement for licensing and practicing the profession as pharmacist. The Pharm. D programme was approved by the Ministry of Health & Family welfare, Govt of India, (Vide letter No: V.13013/1/2007-PMS) announced in gazette of India in 2008, dated the 13th March, 2008.

Vaageswari college of Pharmacy have got its uniqueness in Pharm.D course with centre of excellence.

Vision:

Being an integral member of the healthcare team responsible for the outcomes associated with the medication use process. Contributing to and accepting responsibility for optimal therapeutic outcomes including promoting wellness. Improving the medication use process to enhance continuity of care. Educating patients and healthcare professionals on the safe use of medications.

Mission:

To provide safe, high quality patient care in an atmosphere of professionalism, respect, and effective communication through clinical pharmacy practices.

Principle Strengths of the Department:

  • A blend of experienced and young dynamic staff with diversified exposure in providing clinical pharmacy services and carries out Pharmaceutical research.
  • Good infrastructure with advanced drug information resources to resolve queries related to drugs
  • Our department has good organizational capabilities with computer facilities and is able to plan, prepare and promote continuous professional educational programs in latest drug development and research.
  • Access to global standard drug information resources like American hospital formulary service (AHFS), Thomson Reuters Micromedex and lexi-comp drug information, access to highly reputed Medical and Pharmaceutical journals like Lancet/Elsevier/Springer link journals.
  • Member of Consortium for the Advancement of Medication Information, Policy & Research (CAMIPR) under Division of Drug Information services, University of Iowa, USA.
  • Recognized under Pharmabridge Project of International Pharmaceutical Federation (FIP) for International Students/faculty exchange program.

Department activities:

The professional services offered to health care professionals (Doctor, Nurse, and Community Pharmacist, paramedics) and the general public by the Department of Clinical Pharmacy Practice of Vaageswari college of Pharmacy at-par with International standards:

  • Provision of Drug Information
  • Provision of Poison Information
  • Drug Interaction checking
  • Patient Counselling
  • Drug administration procedures and Techniques
  • Adverse Drug Reaction monitoring and reporting
  • Drug use in special population (Pregnancy and Lactation) etc,
